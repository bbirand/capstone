Amazon.com Analysis Dashboard

Amazon.com has millions of reviews in tens of thousands of product categories. This data can be extremely valuable for market research purposes. In this project, we aim to createa dashboard that brands can use to track their competitors, understand the most important features in a certain category, and discover which product categories are in markets that are underserved by current offerings.
